# Configuration Section  

The files contained in this section are a slightly modified version of the configurations of my printer. I have some optional accessories (buzzer, extra temperature sensors, different stepper motors, etc).  

**PLEASE GO THROUGH THE CONFIGURATION FILES TO MAKE SURE THEY WILL BE COMPATIBLE WITH YOUR SPECIFIC HARDWARE CONFIGURATION**  

> [!WARNING]
> Your CAN UUID's will be different than mine, follow the respective instructions for your toolhead board and mainboard to find yours.
